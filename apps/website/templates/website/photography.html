WELCOME TO THE PHOTOGRAPHY PORTFOLIO

When it comes to photography, I consider myself a dedicated hobbiest. Despite having been in business for almost 6 years, I don't think I'll ever feel 100% comfy calling myself a professional photographer. Don't ask me why. I wouldn't be able to tell ya.

What I CAN tell you is that it has been an amazing 6 years and I don't ever see myself stopping. I can't wait for the nomad year my boyfriend and I are planning so I can add other parts of the world to my little collection.

I describe my photography style as journalistic meets lifestyle. I go back and forth between bright, lively colors and moody edits. In very artistic fashion, my final product tends to tell a story of how I felt while working on my session.

Regardless, thanks for checking out my humble portfolio. Please keep checking back as I have so many pictures to go through to choose to add as well as new sessions to shoot.

If you're interested in booking a session, please head over to the contact page and drop me a line. I would love to hear from you!
