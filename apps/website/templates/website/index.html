What Da F Is
The Jennuine Life?

The Jennuine Life is the home of an always hungry, dreaming, wanderlust-ing, fitnessing, genuine hearted Jennifer Cortes, AKA, ME.

Here, you will find my portfolios for design and photography and you'll also get to read my blog, which just a heads up, will probably be raaaaandom...and very much ME. I'll be talking about random life things, coding, fitness/nutrition, and whatever else pops into my brain

The Jennuine Life is a name and idea that was born years ago, but has taken time to develop...after all, I didn't really know how to get all of ME out there in a somewhat organized manner. But finally, I said "Fuck It" (Yes, I say bad words. Sorry not sorry) and decided to just put it all here, just as it all is and just as I am, because after all, it wouldn't be too genuine if I didn't, huh?
