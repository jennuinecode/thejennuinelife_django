WELCOME TO THE DESIGN PORTFOLIO

To start off, let me just clarify that I am NOT a graphic designer...I have no formal training in that art. What I believe I do have is a knack for Adobe, an eye for good looking things, a passion for creating things on my computer, and branding.

The type of logo design that I do is minimal, font/letter based design. If you're looking for someone to draw you a custom dolphin logo or something, I'm not your girl. If you're wanting something a little more simple, then we'll make a great team.

My true design skills come through web design and coding, which is what I am currently studying and plan on making my career. I love helping people create an online versin of themselves and their business that is a true representation of THEM, much like this site does for me.

As I continue to work on my site, I will continue adding my latest design and web projects, as well as linking to their live counterparts so you can see the websites I have built in action. Until then, I hope you enjoy browing what is currently on here.

If you're interested in working with me, please head over to the contact page and drop me a line. I would love to hear from you!
